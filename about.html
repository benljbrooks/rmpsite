About Us

The story behind Riddle Master Productions

Our Story

Riddle Master Productions was founded by Ben Brooks, a solo developer with extensive experience in the AAA game industry. After years of working on major titles, I decided to pursue my passion for creating thoughtful puzzle games that challenge players' minds while providing an enjoyable experience.

With Riddle Master Productions, I'm focused on crafting games that are accessible yet deep, with mechanics that are easy to learn but difficult to master. By bringing AAA quality standards to indie game development, I aim to create unique gaming experiences that stand out in today's crowded market.
